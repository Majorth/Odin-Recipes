Grilled Salmon with Cilantro Sauce

Description

Salmon is a rich, delictable treat. Adding fresh cilantro with a hint of jalapeno brings a refreshing taste to salmon.

This heart healthy dish will leave wanting to make salmon weekly.

Cooking Information

  • Prep: 10 minutes
  • Cook: 20 minutes
  • Total: 30 minutes
  • Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds of fresh salmon
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 fresh jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
  • Old Bay Seasoning TM to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat grill for hight heat.
  2. Lightly grease one side of a large sheet of aluminum foil.
  3. Place salmon on the greased side of the foil.
  4. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  5. Remove from heat, and mix in cilantro and jalapeno.
  6. When cilantrois wilted, drizzle butter mixture over the salmon.
  7. Place foil with salmon on the grill. Season with Old Bay.
  8. Cook 15 minutes, or until fish is easily flaked with a fork.
  9. Serve with spinach blueberry salad and french bread.
