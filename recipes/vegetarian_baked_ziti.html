Vegetarian Baked Ziti

Description

Vegetarian Baked Ziti is staple for vegetarians. For meat-eaters, it's good to change things up.

This dish is an excellent meatless option for your family with juicy eggplant and zucchini.

Substitue lentil pasta for more protein.

Cooking Information

  • Prep: 30 minutes
  • Cook: 55 minutes
  • Total: 1 hour 25 minutes
  • Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups cubed eggplant
  • 2 cups cubed zucchini
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, cubed
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 (16 ounce) package ziti pasta
  • 1 (32 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) can Italian-style diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup non-diary Parmesan cheese substitute
  • 1 (16 ounce) package sliced fresh mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Spray a 3-quart baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Heat oil in a large saute pan over medium heat.
  4. Add eggplant, zucchini, tomatoes, bell peppers, onion and garlic.
  5. Stir occasionally until vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle with Italian Seasoning and salt. Stir to combine.
  7. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  8. Add ziti and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes.
  9. Drain and rinse in cold water.
  10. Mix spaghetti sauce and diced tomatoes together in a medium bowel.
  11. Spread 1/3 of the sauce mixture in teh bottom of the prepared casserole.
  12. Layer ingredients as follows: 1/2 ziti,
  13. 1/2 vegetable mixture, 1/2 Parmesan, 1/2 mozzarella slices,
  14. 1/2 of the remaining sauce, all of the remaining ziti, all of the vegetables
  15. All the Parmesan and all of the sauce. Set aside the mozzarella slices.
  16. Cover loosely and bake in teh preheated oven until bubbly, 25 to 30 minutes.
  17. Remove from the oven, uncover and top with remaining mozzarella slices.
  18. Return to the oven and bake, uncovered, until cheese is melted and slightly browned, 10 to 15 minutes longer.
  19. Serve with a fresh garden salad and lite Italian dressing.
